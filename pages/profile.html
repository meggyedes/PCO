About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate student embarking on an exciting journey in the world of Information and Communication Technology at HZ University of Applied Sciences.

GitHub LinkedIn Instagram

Personal Information

I’m originally from Herceghalom, Hungary, and on August 1st I moved to Oost-Souburg with my girlfriend. Back in high school, I studied a program focused on computer science, where I gained experience with technologies like PHP, Ajax, and TypeScript. Growing up in the digital age, I’ve always been curious about how things work behind the scenes — from websites and mobile apps to the complex systems that power our modern world.

My journey into ICT began during high school when I first encountered programming. What started as a simple curiosity quickly grew into a genuine passion. I love the logical thinking required in programming, the creativity involved in designing solutions, and the satisfaction of seeing code come to life as functional applications.

Working on cars and motorcycles — whether it’s repairing, tuning, or other mechanical tasks — is my way of taking a mental break from programming. This hands-on work balances out the intensity of coding and gives me a great way to relax and recharge.

In my free time, I like working on small programming projects and experimenting with new technologies. It’s a fun way to put my knowledge into practice while exploring new ideas and creativity.
