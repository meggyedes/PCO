About Me

Hi there! My name is Daniel Soos. Over the past few years, I've been learning web development technologies like HTML, CSS, PHP, and Laravel. I've picked up several other skills along the way. Although I'm still building my experience, I'm always eager to learn something new and improve myself, both professionally and personally.

Personal Information

I am originally from Herceghalom, Hungary, and on August 1st, I moved to Oost-Souburg with my girlfriend. I started learning ICT in secondary school, where the program was focused on ICT. This is where I first tried programming. At first, it was just curiosity, but soon it became my real passion. I enjoy the logical thinking needed in programming, the creativity in designing solutions, and the feeling when code becomes working applications. At the end of school, I earned a professional qualification as a software developer.

During secondary school, I gained comprehensive experience across multiple areas: programming languages (Python, JavaScript, C#, PHP, TypeScript), web technologies (HTML, CSS, React.js, Node.js, Vite, Electron.js, Ajax), frameworks and tools (Laravel, .NET MAUI, Entity Framework), databases (SQL, MariaDB), networking (Packet Tracker), and methodologies (Software Testing, Agility Training). This diverse technical foundation has prepared me well for my continued studies and future career in technology.
