Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HZ University facilities, procedures, and student life. This collection helps me and fellow students navigate university life more effectively.

Printing at HZ is straightforward once you know the process:

  1. Connect to HZ Network: Make sure you're connected to the HZ WiFi network (Eduroam) or use a wired connection.
  2. Install HZ Printers: Go to the HZ IT Service Desk or download the printer drivers from the HZ intranet.
  3. Add Printer: Add the HZ printer to your laptop through your operating system's printer settings.
  4. Print Your Document: Select the HZ printer when printing from any application.
  5. Collect at Printer: Go to any HZ printer, scan your student card, and collect your printed documents.
Tip: You can print from anywhere on campus and collect your documents at any HZ printer using your student card.

Scanning documents at HZ is easy with the multifunction printers:

  1. Find a Multifunction Printer: Locate one of the HZ multifunction printers throughout the campus.
  2. Scan Your Student Card: Use your HZ student card to authenticate at the printer.
  3. Place Your Document: Put your document on the scanner glass or in the document feeder.
  4. Select Scan Option: Choose "Scan to Email" from the printer menu.
  5. Enter Your Email: Type your HZ email address or select it from your profile.
  6. Configure Settings: Choose color/black & white, resolution, and file format (PDF recommended).
  7. Start Scanning: Press the start button and wait for the scan to complete.
  8. Check Your Email: The scanned document will be sent to your email address.
Pro Tip: You can scan multiple pages into one PDF document using the document feeder.

The HZ web shop allows you to purchase various items and services:

  1. Access the Web Shop: Go to the HZ website and navigate to the web shop section.
  2. Log In: Use your HZ student credentials to log into the web shop.
  3. Browse Products: Look through available items such as:
    • Event tickets (like IT introduction events)
    • HZ merchandise
    • Study materials
    • Parking permits
  4. Add to Cart: Select the items you want to purchase and add them to your cart.
  5. Review Order: Check your cart and verify the items and quantities.
  6. Payment: Complete the payment using the available payment methods (usually iDEAL for Dutch students).
  7. Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email with your order details.
Note: Some items may require pickup at specific locations or have delivery timeframes.

Booking project spaces for group work and study sessions:

  1. Access MyHZ Portal: Log into the MyHZ portal using your student credentials.
  2. Navigate to Facilities: Look for the "Facilities" or "Room Booking" section.
  3. Select Room Type: Choose from available project spaces:
    • Small project rooms (2-4 people)
    • Medium project rooms (4-8 people)
    • Large project rooms (8+ people)
    • Specialized rooms with specific equipment
  4. Choose Date and Time: Select your preferred date and time slot.
  5. Check Availability: View available rooms for your selected time.
  6. Book the Room: Confirm your booking and note the room number.
  7. Confirmation: You'll receive a booking confirmation with room details.
Booking Tips: Book in advance for popular time slots, and remember to cancel if you can't make it so others can use the space.

Parking at HZ requires following specific procedures:

For Students:
  1. Register Your Vehicle: Register your car's license plate in the MyHZ portal.
  2. Purchase Parking Permit: Buy a parking permit through the HZ web shop if required.
  3. Designated Areas: Park only in designated student parking areas.
  4. Time Limits: Be aware of any time restrictions for student parking.
  5. Display Permit: If you have a physical permit, display it clearly in your vehicle.
Parking Locations:
  • Main Campus: Student parking areas are clearly marked
  • Visitor Parking: Available for short-term visits
  • Bicycle Parking: Covered bike parking areas available
Important: Unauthorized parking may result in fines. Always check parking signs and regulations.
Eco-Friendly Tip: Consider cycling or using public transport - HZ is well-connected and has excellent bike facilities!
