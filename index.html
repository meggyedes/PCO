Why I Choose ICT

Technology has always fascinated me. From the moment I first wrote a simple "Hello World" program, I knew that the world of Information and Communication Technology was where I belonged. The ability to create solutions, solve complex problems, and build applications that can impact people's lives is what drives my passion for this field.

The HBO-ICT program at HZ University offers the perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills. I'm excited about the opportunity to work on real-world projects, collaborate with fellow students, and learn from experienced professionals in the industry.

My Technical Interests

  • Web Development - Creating responsive and user-friendly websites
  • Mobile App Development - Building applications for iOS and Android
  • Database Management - Organizing and optimizing data storage
  • Cybersecurity - Protecting digital assets and privacy
  • Data Analysis - Extracting insights from complex datasets
