Why I Chose ICT: My Journey to HZ University

Choosing a study program is one of the most important decisions in life. Here's my journey of discovering my passion for technology and why the ICT program at HZ University was the perfect fit.

The Spark of Interest

My interest in technology started when I was young. I remember being fascinated by how computers work, how websites are created, and how mobile apps can solve real problems. My grandparents were both programmers a long time ago. My grandmother often told me stories about the exciting projects they worked on, and my grandfather shared many stories about his programming career when I was little. What began as curiosity slowly grew into a real passion for understanding and creating digital solutions.

During secondary school, I had my first chance to learn about programming in a computer science class. Writing my first "Hello World" program might seem simple to many people, but for me, it was a very important moment. Being able to tell a computer what to do and see the result immediately felt very exciting and powerful. I realized that I had found something special.

Exploring My Options

When it came time to choose a study program, I researched various options in the technology field. I considered:

  • Computer Science at University: More theoretical and research-focused
  • Software Engineering: Highly specialized in programming
  • HBO-ICT: Practical, industry-focused with broad ICT knowledge
  • Applied Sciences: Hands-on approach with real-world applications

Why HBO-ICT at HZ University?

After thorough research and thinking, I found many strong reasons why HBO-ICT at HZ University is the perfect choice for me. This program offers a balanced mix of practical experience, theoretical knowledge, and personal development that prepares students well for the fast-changing world of ICT.

1. Practical, Hands-On Learning

The HBO-ICT program focuses strongly on practical skills and real-world projects. Instead of only reading about theory, students get to work on assignments that are very close to actual problems faced by companies today. This means I can gain real experience and learn how to solve problems in a professional way. The program uses real clients and projects from local and international partners, which gives me a real taste of what working in ICT is like.

Additionally, the program encourages agile working methods. This means working flexibly with a team, adjusting goals as needed, and focusing on delivering results in small, manageable steps. This approach helps students develop teamwork skills and learn how to manage projects efficiently — skills important for any ICT professional.

2. Strong Industry Connections

HZ University has excellent partnerships with a wide range of companies both locally and internationally. These connections create many opportunities for internships, guest lectures from industry experts, and potential job offers after graduation. The university organizes events and workshops where students can meet professionals and learn about the latest trends in technology. This close relationship between education and industry ensures that students stay up-to-date with rapidly evolving ICT developments.

Furthermore, students can often work on real business challenges as part of their assignments. Being exposed to these challenges early helps build confidence and valuable experience that companies look for in future employees.

3. Comprehensive and Diverse Curriculum

The HBO-ICT curriculum covers a wide range of important subjects, making graduates versatile and ready to enter different ICT roles. The program includes:

  • Web and mobile application development, where students learn to create functional and user-friendly apps;
  • Database design and management to handle large amounts of data efficiently;
  • Network administration and cybersecurity, focusing on protecting systems and ensuring reliable communication;
  • Project management and teamwork skills, which prepare students to lead or participate in complex projects;
  • Business analysis and communication, helping students understand client needs and work effectively within teams.

Students can choose specializations in the later years, such as Software Engineering, Data Science, or Business IT Consulting, allowing them to pursue their interests and career goals more closely.

4. International and Diverse Learning Environment

HZ University attracts students from many countries, creating a diverse and international learning atmosphere. This is very valuable in the global ICT industry, where working with people from different cultures is common. This environment encourages understanding and collaboration between students from different backgrounds, preparing them for the multicultural workplaces they will enter after graduation.

Additionally, courses and projects often include international perspectives and teamwork, which helps students build skills relevant to global markets. This international focus shows HZ University’s commitment to not just teaching technology but also developing global citizens ready for the future.

My Career Aspirations

Looking ahead, I see myself working in the dynamic field of web development or mobile app development. The ICT industry offers incredible opportunities for innovation and creativity. I'm particularly interested in:

  • Creating user-friendly applications that solve real problems
  • Working with cutting-edge technologies and frameworks
  • Collaborating with diverse teams on challenging projects
  • Potentially starting my own tech company in the future

What I Hope to Achieve

Through the HBO-ICT program, I aim to:

  • Develop strong technical skills in multiple programming languages
  • Learn to work effectively in team environments
  • Understand the business side of technology
  • Build a portfolio of impressive projects
  • Establish a network of professional connections
  • Gain practical experience through internships

The Journey Ahead

I'm excited about the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. The ICT field is constantly evolving, which means there will always be new technologies to learn and master. This continuous learning aspect is something I find particularly appealing – it ensures that my career will never become stagnant or boring.

The first few weeks at HZ University have already exceeded my expectations. The quality of instruction, the modern facilities, and the enthusiasm of both students and faculty create an inspiring learning environment. I'm confident that I've made the right choice.

Conclusion

Choosing to study HBO-ICT at HZ University wasn't just about following a career path – it was about pursuing my passion and positioning myself for a future in one of the most exciting and rapidly growing industries in the world. Every day brings new learning opportunities, and I'm grateful to be on this journey.

For anyone considering a similar path, my advice is to follow your interests, research your options thoroughly, and don't be afraid to take on the challenge. The ICT field needs passionate, dedicated individuals who are ready to innovate and solve tomorrow's problems.
